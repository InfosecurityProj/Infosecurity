Welcome To Devil's Kitchen Restaurant

Little Story

Tom Sellers is the Chef Patron of Devil's Kitchen Restaurant. He dreamed of opening Devil's Kitchen Restaurant at the age of 19, and in April 2013, aged 26, was able to realise his dream. Tom trained at some of the world’s best restaurants including Restaurant Tom Aikens, Noma and Per Se.

His food is predominantly British, using seasonality to lead a menu that invokes memory and brings a narrative to each plate.
