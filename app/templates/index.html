Welcome To HoHo's Hotel

Little Story

The founder of HoHo's Hotel is a 30-year-old named Hovan, who dreamed of opening his own hotel/restaurant business. He founded HoHo's Hotel on March 2023 which was a great accomplishment of his. He started having this dream back when he was 20 years old. To achieve this dream, he studied overseas at China to learn business management for 10 years.

After coming back to Singapore, he used his money that he saved up from doing part-time jobs during his time of studying to open this hotel.
